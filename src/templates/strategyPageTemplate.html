Strategic Horizon

Our Mission

To catalyze sustainable peace by identifying, funding, and scaling innovative peacebuilding solutions that address the root causes of conflict at the intersection of technology and human rights.

Our Vision

A world where peace is not merely the absence of war, but the presence of justice, equity, and the flourishing of human potential across all borders.

Dr. Elena Thorne
